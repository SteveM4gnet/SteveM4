About me

Hi, I'm Steve.

I like creating content on the Internet, and this is a website I have made myself where I can show off what I have made.

My main passions are Music production, web development, 3D Modelling and of course, trains.
This isn't a CV, it's a piece of text on the internet about myself.

Music production

The music I produce can often be called 'Electronic', because its made on a computer. Beyond that, classifying it under a label or genre is difficult. One month I can be making a track like Control and other times I can make stuff like Aquarossa.

I have been producing music under various aliases for about 7 years now, and I believe I have acquired a unique style and flair in my production. You may have known me as Mush, Kloizta or SplintR, but none of these felt right. I have now come to unify my 'brand' as SteveM4. Bit more about that later.

Web Development

I started Web Development mid 2018 and, to be honest, I sucked at it. I have been teaching myself since and the end result of that is what you are seeing infront of you now.

I have done other projects for other businesses in the past, and this is my first personal project where I have complete control. The blue colour I like so much is hex #3D7FAF.

3D Modelling and Modding

I began modding games back in the late '00s when I found out you could mess with the files from Chris Sawyer's Locomotion using an old tool from 2004 called LocoTool to take apart the .dat files.

From there, I learned that most PC games (from that era) could be tweaked locally. I started messing with Transport Tycoon, RollerCoaster Tycoon 2 and Robot Arena 2 to name a few.

Later on, in 2018, is when I picked this up again with Transport Fever from Urban Games. I have known for a while how to mess with the local files, give engines more power or adjust the stats. However, I realised I could modify the models too if I learned Blender, and this is where my 3D Modelling hobby started

So far, I have really only made mods for Transport Fever, and they are all nearly exclusively steam locomotives from the UK (Bar one USA pack and a few diesels). I love moddlling and modding these engines as I learn a lot about the history and design of the locomotives

And that brings me nicely on too...

TRAINS

I like trains.

As you can see by my icons (that isn't the M4 logo), I do rather like the puffers. My love of trains goes back all the way back to one time when my late Grandpa took me to the Bluebell Railway in Sussex, and I saw my first full size steam locomotive.

If you are wondering, the little engine in the icon is Lively Polly, a tiny 0-4-0WT that ran around on the old Liverpool Overhead Railway, doing some shunts and whatnot.

If you need to know anything about steam locomotives, I am your guy.

Branding

I have recently undergone a huge rebrand to make everything uniform.

My YouTube has been renamed from SplintR, also my SoundCloud and secondary Twitter has been re-branded and re-launched under SteveM4.

The branding is really simple. Blue with white text. The blue is #3D7FAF and the font is Comfortaa on pure white. The logo is three white upside down piano keys on the blue with a white 4, can be inverted.

My secondary branding for personal Twitter and YouTube where I post history things is Lively Polly, with the banner image being whatever I find good at that point.
